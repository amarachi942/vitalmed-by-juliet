Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is VitalMed?

  • VitalMed is a telemedicine platform that connects patients with certified doctors for online consultations. We also provide prescription services and deliver medications to your doorstep.

    • 2. How do I book an appointment?

  • To book an appointment: 1. Sign in to your account. 2. Select your preferred doctor and service. 3. Choose an available appointment time. 4. Confirm your appointment and wait for the notification when it’s time for your video consultation.

    • 3. Do I need a hospital card to book an appointment?

  • Yes, a hospital card is required for booking an appointment. If you don't have one, you can request one through our platform during the appointment booking process.

    • 4. How do I get a hospital card?

  • If you don’t have a hospital card, you can request one while booking your first appointment. After making the required payment, your hospital card ID will be sent to your email.

    • 5. How does the video consultation work?

  • Once you book an appointment, you will receive a notification before the scheduled time. Simply log in and click on the link provided to start the video consultation with the doctor.

    • 6. What if I miss my scheduled appointment?

  • If you miss your appointment, you can reschedule by logging into your account and selecting a new time or contacting our support team for assistance.

    • 7.How do I get my prescription after the consultation?

  • After your consultation, the doctor will issue a prescription, which you can view in your account. You can choose to have the medication delivered to your address through our delivery service.

    • 8. What are the payment options available?

  • We accept various payment methods, including: - Online bank transfer - USSD payment - Mobile banking You can select your preferred method at checkout when requesting services or medications.

    • 9. How does delivery work?

  • VitalMed offers two delivery options:
    - *Standard Delivery*: Takes 2-3 business days.
    - *Express Delivery*: Medication is delivered within 24 hours.
    • Simply choose your delivery option during checkout, and your medications will be delivered to your specified address.

    10. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

  • Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment by logging into your account and selecting the "Cancel" or "Reschedule" option under your appointment details.

    • 11. Is my personal and medical information secure?

  • At VitalMed, we prioritize the security of your personal and medical information. Our platform is encrypted and complies with all necessary privacy regulations to protect your data.

    • 12 How can I contact customer support?

  • You can reach our customer support team via email at okoligweamarachi9@gmail.com or call us at +234 8138490179. We’re available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries or issues.
